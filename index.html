Afternoon games online

  • Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday

  • Game starts about 1 PM

  • Members may play with guests
  • Don’t get booted. Be active on BBO in the minutes before game time.

Evening games online

  • Tuesday, Thursday
  • Registration opens about 2 PM – BBO Login page
  • Game starts about 4 PM
  • 7 rounds, 2 boards/round, 7 minutes/board, leisurely pace
  • Members may play with guests
  • Don’t get booted. Be active on BBO in the minutes before game time.
